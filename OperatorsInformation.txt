C# operators and thier precedence closely resemble the operators in other langauges
of the C family.
Similar to C++, classes can overload most operators, defining or redefining the 
behavior of the operators in contexts where the first argument of that operator 
is an instance of that class, 
but doing so is often discouraged
for clarity.

Operators can be grouped by thier ARITY as UNARY and BINARY
